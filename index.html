About Us

Jhal NYC is a social entrepreneurship venture built to empower the Bangali New York community through food. We employ stay at home mothers and new immigrants, help build their language skills, and prepare them for careers they seek to transition to. More than anything, we are creating a community for these marginalized groups.



Jhal NYC was created to shine light on the distinct Bangali- New York culture that we grew up in. The taste of the Bangali food we were raised on, the aesthetic and sound of the streets of New York. We adorn our presence with the faces of those like Tagore and Kazi Nazrul because their words are the ones our family sung.




It's more than just about food, it's us.

We in the Bengali newspaper!

"Jhal is a word that makes most Bengali's mouth water. There are very few Bengali people who do not like spicy food. In this modern era, when foreign food restaurants fill our cities, we are on our way to forget our own food culture. While we are busy conforming to Western culture, thousands of miles away from Bangladesh some young Americans, thought of something unique. In New York City they established an organization called Jhal NYC. Jhal NYC has been created to empower and unite the Bengali-American community. This organization employs Bengali-American housewives and new immigrants and puts them on a pathway to higher education or a career, while helping them adjust to the American society. In addition, Jhal NYC is a food vendor that serves authentic Bengali food. Jhal NYC attended various events in New York such as LIC Flea & Food, Astoria Night Market, Queens Night Market, Boogie on the Boulevard in the Bronx, and the Transition Photo Exhibition, which has earned them a reputation for their food. Jhal NYC seeks to flag the Bengali identity in American land." #ifyouaintbanginyouaintbangali #news #newspaper #bengali #newyork #bangladesh #icapture_nyc #newyork_ig #loves_nyc #seeyourcity #newyork_instagram #ig_nycity #what_i_saw_in_nyc #nycdotgram #nycprimeshot #nyc_explorers #igersnyc #thosenewyorkstreets #instagramnyc #Igersnewyork #abc7ny #nbc4ny #fox5ny #just_newyork #nyc_uncut
